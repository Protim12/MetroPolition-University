      Programme Summary

The B.Sc. Engineering (CSE) programme is a 48 (forty-eight)-month study of 12 semesters for regular students. A student should complete at least 150credits to be a graduate. The program is intended for students who want to obtain a sound theoretical and technical knowledge in Computer Science. The job market in computer Science and Information Technology is robust and growing in a geometric profession in Bangladesh and throughout the world. Over the next several decades, Computer Scientist will continue to reshape our familiar world.
